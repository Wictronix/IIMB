Welcome To Unmaad 2025

IIM Bangalore's "Unmaad 2025" is one of India's largest business school cultural festivals, happening offline from Feb 21-23, 2025. It offers a variety of competitions such as street dramas, music, dance, quizzes, and debates, culminating in a grand fashion show.

The festival has featured famous artists like Mohit Chauhan, Shankar Ehsaan Loy, and Prateek Kuhad, attracting attendees nationwide.
